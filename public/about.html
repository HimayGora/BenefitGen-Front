About BenefitGen™

The Problem: Engineering teams spend hours rewriting technical updates for business stakeholders. "We migrated our API" becomes three different emails—one for executives focused on cost savings, one for product teams focused on user impact, and one for engineering teams focused on implementation details.

Our Solution: BenefitGen automatically generates stakeholder-appropriate summaries from your technical work. Paste your feature description or technical update, get three tailored outputs in seconds—no more manual translation between engineering language and business language.
