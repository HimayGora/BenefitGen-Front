Important Usage Guidance

BenefitGen™ is designed for safe, non-sensitive use cases like translating technical product docs into business language for clearer stakeholder communication. It is not intended for personally identifiable information (PII), proprietary source code, legal documents, or other confidential material. For more sensitive use cases, contact us to discuss custom terms or private deployments. Uploads are retained only as needed for processing and per your subscription plan.
