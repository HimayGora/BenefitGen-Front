BenefitGen™ - Executive Business Report Generator

What BenefitGen™ Does

BenefitGen transforms technical documentation into concise, stakeholder-ready business reports. It instantly creates multi-output cards summarizing APIs, database plans, and technical debt into executive briefings.

Key Features

  • Generate 3 stakeholder-ready summaries from a single technical input.
  • Supports copy-paste or typed inputs with planned upload support for text, markdown, readme, and changelog files.
  • Instant multi-output cards delivered in seconds.
  • Audience-specific drafting coming soon (execs, PMs, tech leads, consumers).
  • Export options including Word, PowerPoint, and email formats (planned).

Pricing Plans

  • Free Plan: 40 cards per month, no credit card required.
  • Pro Plan: $35/month for 450 cards and up to 1000 word inputs with export features.
  • Community Plan (Upcoming): Available to US users (excluding California), opt-in plan offering up to 200 cards with I/O data sharing and early access rewards.

Why Choose BenefitGen™?

Save time and improve communication by converting complex technical docs into clear, business-friendly reports that stakeholders can understand and act upon. Perfect for teams wanting to bridge the gap between technical and non-technical stakeholders efficiently.
